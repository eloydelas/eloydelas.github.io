Professional Summary

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher with a multidisciplinary background in Chemical Engineering, Biomedical Engineering, and Medicine. For over a decade, my research has focused on developing advanced MRI analysis methods to identify quantitative biomarkers for Multiple Sclerosis (MS) and other neuroimmunological diseases.

My key contributions include characterizing subtle white/gray matter changes, conducting brain network analysis, and applying machine learning for predictive disease modeling. A major technical achievement was leading the implementation of the XNAT platform for automated MRI processing.
